About Me

To begin a short summary of how I became interested in web development, I will give two fun facts about myself:

I like dogs.

I am a graduate from the University of Arizona.

I stumbled upon the U of A Coding Boot Camp and immediately enrolled. From there I have not looked back. I am both excited and challenged by the amount of information and content we are exploring. I have a desire to learn and have affirmed, that although I'm not proficient now, I will try to master this skill set.
